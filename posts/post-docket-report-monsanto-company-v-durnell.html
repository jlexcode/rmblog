Docket Report: Monsanto Company v. Durnell

June 24, 2026 • jed
Note: I am catching up on cases argued since April and descriptions will be (very) brief and incomplete. Predictions based on the same model used for other cases. Question of whether the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) preempts state-law failure-to-warn claims over Roundup when the EPA-approved label does not require a cancer warning and Monsanto says it could not add one without EPA approval. Missouri courts rejected Monsanto’s preemption argument. The model predicts the Court will reverse.
